Hangman

×

This is a Hangman game based on cult classic movie titles. The game utilized HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The game picks a random title from an array, after the first wrong guess the user gets a hint containing a quote from the movie. If the movie is guessed correctly an image from the movie comes up. If the movie title is guessed incorectly the answer is given after all 8 tries to guess have been exhausted.

scroll

×

Scroll is a full stack social media app that allows writers to post their work and gain a following without having to go through the publisher. Scroll was built in a group of two people.

Scroll was an incredible learning experience as I got to work on all the aspects of the app. We have 3 collections , author, post and comment and all three have to have access to each other's information. Working with Mongoose and figuring most efficient queries was both, challenging and rewarding. I also worked with Express to define routes for adding and retrieving information from the datatbase. I sketched and coded the UX/UI elements of the app as well.
